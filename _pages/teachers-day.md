The last day of the conference will be dedicated to a mutual exchange of ideas between researchers and teachers. The program is under development, but we plan:
  - A presentation of the local Montessori primary school on teaching math and computational thinking with the Montessori approach.
  - For a select number of accepted papers, authors will be allowed to present their work to teachers in the form of laboratories, workshops, or tutorials.
  - Other presentations by invited speakers.

Stay tuned!
