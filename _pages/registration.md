The registration fees for CMSC are the following:
- Early registation 155€ until August 28, 2026.
- Late registration 200€ from August 29, 2026.

Link: to be added.

The registation includes: attendance to the conference and to the teachers'day, coffe breaks, 
social event (visit to Palazzo Bo and Padova city center), and social dinner. Lunches and other dinners are not included.

It is possible to include the participation of an accompanying person to the social event and dinner at an additional cost of 65€.

Due to organization constraings, please note the follows:
- At least on author of accepted papers is required to register by July 31st, 2026.
- Confirm your attendance at the teachers day during the registration process.
- It would not be possible to register in situ.
