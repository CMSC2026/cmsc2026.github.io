The registration fees for CMSC 2026 are the following:
- Early registration 155€ until August 28, 2026.
- Late registration 200€ from August 29, 2026.

Link: to be added.

The registration includes:
- Attendance to the conference and to the Teachers' Day;
- Coffee breaks;
- Social event (visit to Palazzo Bo and Padova city center);
- Social dinner.
Lunches and other dinners are **not** included.

It is possible to include the participation of an accompanying person to the social event and dinner at an additional cost of 65€.

Due to organization constraints, please note the followings:
- At least one author of accepted papers is required to register by July 31st, 2026.
- Attendance at the Teachers' Day must be indicated uring the registration process.
- It will not be possible to register in situ.
